Shipping & Logistics

Reliable worldwide export from Incheon and Busan.

Ro-Ro Shipping (Roll-on/Roll-off)

The most common and cost-effective method for shipping vehicles. Cars are driven directly onto the shipping vessel and secured inside the weather-proof cargo area.

  • Best for standard sized passenger cars, SUVs, and trucks.
  • Requires the vehicle to be in full running condition.
  • Personal items cannot be left inside the vehicle.

Container Shipping (20ft / 40ft / 40HC)

Ideal for high-value luxury cars, multiple vehicle shipments, or when shipping additional spare parts/accessories along with the car.

  • Maximum security and protection from the elements.
  • Allows for additional auto parts to be loaded.
  • Can fit 2-4 cars per 40ft container, reducing cost per unit.

Departure Ports

We operate primarily out of the two largest export hubs in South Korea:

Incheon Port

The major hub for used car exports, located near Seoul. Perfect for RoRo shipments to the Middle East, Central Asia, and North Africa.

Busan Port

The primary container port in Korea. Ideal for containerized shipments globally, including Europe, South America, and Oceania.
