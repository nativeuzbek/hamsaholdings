About Hamsa Holdings

Bridging Korea's Auto Market to the World

Founded and operated in South Korea for over 10 years, Hamsa Holdings has grown from a local dealership partner into a premier automotive exporter. We export brand-new models, high-quality pre-owned vehicles, and budget-friendly options directly from Korea's leading distribution hubs.

Our company represents integrity and trust. We do not just source vehicles; we provide a complete service cycle: diagnostics, repair, detailing, local paperwork processing, port delivery, and safe oceanic shipping. We have established relationships with primary shipping lines to ensure priority boarding and competitive ocean freights for our international clients.

Licensed & Registered

Operating fully under South Korean corporate laws with absolute legal transparency.

Global Delivery Reach

Regular export corridors to Central Asia, Middle East, Europe, South America, and Africa.
