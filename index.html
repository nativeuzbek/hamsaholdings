How It Works

Our Simple Export Process

From selecting your vehicle to custom clearance in your country, we handle everything with care and professional precision.

1

Choose Your Vehicle

Select from our catalog or send us your specific brand, model, year, and budget requirements for a custom search.

2

Get a Full Quotation

We provide a transparent FOB or CIF price quotation, including inspection fees, logistics, shipping rates, and port terminal handling.

3

150-Point Inspection & Payment

We inspect the vehicle thoroughly, providing high-resolution images/videos. Once you approve, payment is processed securely via bank telegraphic transfer (T/T).

4

Shipment & Port Handover

The vehicle is cleaned, securely packed (or loaded on RORO), and delivered to Incheon/Busan port. Export declaration and custom clearance in Korea are processed.

5

Receive Documents & Car

We mail the original Bill of Lading (B/L), Export Certificate, Commercial Invoice, and Packing List via DHL so you can easily clear your car at destination.
